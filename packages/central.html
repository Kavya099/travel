Itinerary

In order to travel through some of the most attractive places in the centre of India, you first have to reach Delhi Airport till afternoon. Here our representatives will recieve you and talotus templeke you to the recommended hotel and will help you out in whole check-in process. On the first day of this tour we will provide you enough time and space for relaxation at the hotel where an overnight stay is scheduled for you.

On the every next day of your tour, the national capital of India awaits you for a pleasent visit. With our tour package, you will be travelling throughout the city which is divided in two parts, New and Old Delhi. Here at New Delhi, you will get to visit some well known historical monuments of India like Humayun's Tomb, Qutub Minar, India Gate, Parliament House, Secretariat Building, The Connought Place and Laxminarayan Temple. While on the other side, the Old Delhi will show you the Red Fort, Chandni Chowk, Shanti Van and Rajghat. All over the day, you will also get enough time for fulfilling your shopping desires.

After staying in Delhi hotel overnight, we will schedule your next day travelling to Jaipur. This is also known as the pink city of Rajasthan and is 260 Km far from New Delhi. As we will take you at this destination by road, it will be seen as a whole day journey. After arriving at Jaipur by evening, we will arrange a good hotel for you and will check you in to your respective rooms. Here you will be having an overnight stay.

On the fourth day of your tour, you will get to see the evergrowing beauty of Jaipur from the morning itself. Here Amer Fort is seen as one of the major attractions. A blend of Hindu - Muslim architecture can be seen in this fort. This fort also carries some interesting apartments in its complex. The other places in the city like Hawa Mahal, Ram Niwas Gardens and Observatory gives you the mixed pleasure of history and royalty.

The fifth day of this tour takes to further to the centre of India. Here you will be travelling by road from Jaipur to Agra through the 6 hrs long drive. However, the day is not all empty with this travelling as we will let you see Fatehpur Sikri in between the way. The place is famous as once the Emporer Akbar visited here to pray for his son. Other attractions like Panch Mahal, Mosque of Salim Chisti and other palaces makes the place more remarkable.

Finally, on the sixth day of your tour, we will take you to the symbol of love Taj Mahal, which stands first in the race of seven wonders of this world. This monument was build by Shahjahan in the memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It attracts millions of tourists from all over the world every year. However, this is not the only attractive spot Agra carries in its heart. Tomb of Itimad-ud-Dualah and Red Fort of Agra are also seen as some pleasing tourist spots of the country.

Next bright day of the tour is scheduled for you to take a train ride from Agra to Jhansi. Here, our representatives will help you out in taking a comfortable reach to the train. After reaching Jhansi, we will take you to the visit of Orcha. Orcha is known as one of the most largest and powerful kingdoms of central India. The architecture and heritage full places deserves a watch for the whole day. The main attractions here involves Raj Mahal, Jehangir Mahal, Raj Praveen Mahal, Chaturbhuj Temple, Ram Raj Temple and Sunder Mahal.

On the eighth day of this package you will find yourself standing in the city of Khajuraho, which carries many emotions and suspense in its heart. The temples constructed here from 950 to 1050 AD explains the story of Indo-Aryan architecture. The images of gods and godesses, real and mythological animals, warriors and musicians as well as the images of women and sex from the story of Kamasutra acts as most eye catching spots for tourists.

We will let you sail through the whole city in boat through which you can grab every action happening here in the city's mythological temples and spots.

The eleventh day will mark the end of this tour when we will take you to Sarnath which is only 7 Km away from Varanasi. After visiting this holy place you will be transferred to Delhi for your onwards journey.
