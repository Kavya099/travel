15 NIGHTS / 16 DAYS - WILDLIFE AND CULTURE TOUR

Destination

Delhi - Agra - Jaipur - Udaipur - Jodhpur - Jaisalmer - Bikaner - Mandawa

Wildlife and religion are two different points of views of seeing India but it is the only country which can give you the pleasure of wildlife safari and religious temple visit in one tour package. Here, we have planned out a 16 day tour package which will take you to the well known wildlife sanctuaries and temples of India simultaneously.

Itinerary

Delhi is the capital city of India and in house some great heritage monuments of the bygone era. Detailed Itinerary : The wildlife and culture tour will start with your arrival in the national capital of India, Delhi. Here you will arrive at the Indira Gandhi International Airport and our representatives will recieve you and take you to a comfortable hotel, where you will be spending the night.

On the second day of your tour, we will give you enough time for relaxation and afterwards we will take you to the memorable visit of Delhi. The city is divided in two parts, one is Old Delhi where the historical places like Red Fort and more than 150 years old market Chandni Chowk grabs your attention. You will be given enough time and space to fulfill your shopping desires. Coming to New Delhi, you will experience a totally new era. Here the places like President's House, Parliament House, Qutub Minar and Humayun's Tomb plays a central role in catching your attraction. Your day will end when you will arrive in your same Delhi hotel for an overnight stay.

On the third day, you will be packing your bags to leave the national capital for arriving in the pink city of Rajasthan, Jaipur. This city gives you a feel of royal history and hertitage with its monuments like City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jaigarh Fort and Nahargarh Fort. We will make you visit all these attractions, just after checking you in a comfortable hotel in the same day. Further, we will also take you to very famous Shilpgram. This is actually a craft market where Rajasthan's artistic beauty attracts many tourists at higher level.

Your fourth tour day will mark your journey in Jaipur, which will start with your visit to Amber Fort through an elephant ride. This place resembles the Rajasthan history in its art and architecture. Further we will take you to the Ranthambore National Park, which is a well known wildlife sanctuary of the country. Here, we will provide you a comfortable overnight stay at a recommended hotel.

Ranthambore National Park is a famous tiger reserve of Rajasthan which was once noted as a hunting ground of maharajas of Jaipur. You will visit this place on the fifth day of your tour. This wildlife sanctuary is spread in an area of 274.5 sq km.

Moving forward for more wildlife exploration, next we will reach the Bharatpur Birds Sanctuary. This place exhibits more than 300 species of birds including egrets, open bill storks, black headed ibis and grey herons. Some other animals like Sambar, Nilgai, Jackals, Deers, Wild Boars, Jackals and Jungle Cats are also spotted here. With us, you can also enjoy a boat ride in the birds sanctuary, which will take you directly to Kaledeoghana National Park for another magnificent wildlife experience.

After experiencing the Bharatpur Wildlife Sanctuary we will move towards Agra, where an overnight stay is scheduled for you.

Situated in North India, Agra is a scenic site which houses the famous Taj Mahal - a World Heritage Site. This small city of Madhya Pradesh is quite famous for Khajuraho temples. After completing the Agra journey, you will move forward to reach Khajuraho. This place is mainly known as temple city of central India. It is famous for its temples which explain the history of Kamasutra through its art and architecture on walls. Khajuraho was also ruled by Rajputs.

Further on the tenth day of your tour, you will drive forward to reach the Bandhavgarh National Park. It takes about 6 hours to reach here from Khajuraho. This forest reserve is situated in Madhya Pradesh and is famous for the white tigers which have been on the cover page of some best wildlife magazines throughout the world. Bandhavgarh Fort also stands as one main attraction of this place. After visiting these places we will arrange an overnight stay at a comfortable hotel for you.

The eleventh day will mark your jungle safari in the Bandhavgarh National Park. You can chose between elephant or a jeep safari. This journey gives you an extreme chance of seeing a tiger here. After this two hour long safari, you will be coming back to your resort for the overnight stay.

On the twelfth day of your tour, you will get to visit the famous Kanha National Park. Here also, the option of jeep safari is available which let you have a close look at rarely seen tigers. Jeeps for these safaris can be hired from MTDC hotels for mornings and afternoon visits. Besides this, the Kanha National Park is also famous for the Kanha Museum, which tells the historical tales related to this place.

The thirteenth day of your tour will be scheduled for another visit to Kanha National Park. Here we will give you an ultimate experience of birds sanctuary which is only possible in the core area. Further, an overnight stay in the same hotel will be scheduled for you.

Your fourteenth tour day will also be spent in the Kanha National Park where an another tiger spotting safari will be scheduled for you.

After visiting all the all the famous wildlife spots, we will take you to Nagpur, which is noted as the second capital of Madhya Pradesh and is also famous for its oranges. After spending half day here, you will reach a recommended hotel for an overnight stay.

The sixteenth day will be the last day of your tour when you will take a flight from Nagpur to Delhi and move for your onwards destination.
