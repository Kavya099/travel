12 NIGHTS / 13 DAYS - NORTH INDIA TOUR WITH GOA BEACH

Destination

Delhi - Varanasi - Khajuraho - Orchha - Jhansi - Agra - Jaipur - Udaipur - Mumbai - Goa - Mumbai

There are many tourist places present in the world which have some well described speciality uncommon to any other nation. But India is a bit ahead in this race as it offers its tourists with much more uncommon things. Yes, that’s right. Otherwise where else will you find the beauty of backwaters along with spice gardens and a topping of eye-catching sunrise and sunset views. That’s Kerala for you, the God’s Own Country, where the south side beauty is so much visible in its sights, historical museums, palaces, wild life sanctuaries and tea plantations and backwaters, that ten days look too small for a mind blowing visit.

Itinerary

Your North India and Beach Tour will commence at the Delhi, which is noted as the national capital of India. Here, you will be starting your journey by arriving at the International Airport. Further, our representatives will receive you and take you to the hotel as per your comfort. You will be spending whole night in the same hotel.

Full day visit Old Delhi and New Delhi. OLD DELHI : An ancient walled city. Here you will see Red Fort, the most opulent Fort and Palace of the Mughal Empire: Raj Ghat, the memo -rial site of the Mahatma Gandhi; Jama Masjid, the largest mosque in India; and Chandni Chowk, the bustling and colourful market of the old city. NEW DELHI : This city designed and built by the British in the 1920's - a city of wide boulevards impressive Government buildings, green parks and gardens. First you will visit two monuments from Delhi's past Humayun's Tomb and Qutub Minar. Then your drive takes you along the ceremonial avenue, Rajpath, past the imposing India Gate and Parliament House. Overnight at the hotel.

Third day of your tour will be scheduled for your journey from Delhi to Varanasi. Here, you will be reaching the airport in order to catch the early flight to Varanasi. Varanasi is a place full of religion and temples and is situated on the bank of holy River Ganges. This place is very much known and famous in all over the world for its temples dedicated to Lord Shiva and many other Gods and Goddesses. Varanasi mostly catches one’s attention with its boat ride tourism where the pleasure of visiting almost each and every famous temple is provided through a boat ride throughout River Ganges. Here, we will take you to a recommended hotel for an overnight stay.

Your fourth tour day will start with the boat ride in Varanasi, through which you will be visiting the places like Daswamedh Ghats and Sunrise Point. After visiting both of the two places we will take you back to the hotel for a comfortable breakfast session. Further in the same day, you will also be visiting Bharat Mata Temple, Tulsi Manas Temple, Art Gallery and Mosque of Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb. In the second half of the day, you will be reaching the airport to catch the earliest flight to Khajuraho. The place is famous for its ancient art which is exhibited on the walls of its temples. The paintings, statues, art, structure and crafts of historical era, explains the story of Kamasutra also.

On the fifth day of your tour, we will take you to the visit of main Khajuraho temples, just after the breakfast at Khajuraho hotel. This visit will also include Eastern Temples and Jain Temples. Further in the same day we will move to Orchha by road. This place exhibits the temples built in 16th and 17th century and is situated near River Betwa. Fort Complex is also one main attraction here. In the second half of the day, we will be reaching Agra, which is famous for Taj Mahal and many other ancient buildings and forts dedicated to Mughal Era. Here, you will be reaching a recommended hotel for an overnight stay.

Your sixth day of package is scheduled for the Agra visit. Firstly, we will move to the Taj Mahal, which is also noted as most beautiful wonder of the world. This was built by Shahajahan in the memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Agra Fort is another tourist attraction of the city. This place was built by Mughal Emperor Akbar in 1565 AD and is also having a Pearl Mosque inside it. The day will also include your visit to Fatehpur Sikri.

After having the breakfast in Agra hotel, we will take you to the rest of those Agra tourist attraction which are not covered in the previous day. After getting done with Agra, we will move to Jaipur. This place is also known as pink city of Rajasthan and is full of royal heritage and modernisation. The places like Amber Fort and Hawa Mahal are its main attractions where you can find the glimpse of Rajasthani architecture and crafts in its interiors and exteriors. Here, we will provide you a comfortable overnight stay at a recommended hotel

On the eighth day of your tour, you will open your eyes to see the beautiful city Jaipur from your point of view. One by one we will take you to the places like Amber Fort which was built by Man Singh in the year 1592. Further we will take you to City Palace where to the top attractions of the city like City Palace Museum, Shri Govind Dev Temple and Chandra Mahal are also involved. A visit to Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar will also be scheduled on the same day. Further, you will be having an overnight stay in the same Jaipur Hotel.

After having the breakfast in Jaipur hotel, your ninth day will be scheduled for a 6 hr journey from Jaipur to Udaipur. Udaipur is also known as the lake city of Rajasthan and exhibits the royal living in the back drops of deserts. After reaching here, we will provide you a comfortable overnight stay in a recommended hotel.

The tenth day of this tour is also dedicated to Udaipur. In this day we will take you to some eye catching places for sightseeing and will end the day after visiting the well known attractive places like City Palace, City Palace Museum, Shelion ki Bari, Jagdish Temple, Bharatiya Lok Kala Mandir and Pratap Memorial. One thing we want you to remember that just like another excellent tour day with us, we provide full facility of lunch, dinner and enough shopping space and time to you. Finally, we will close this day at Udaipur hotel where we will arrange a comfortable overnight stay for you.

Finally on the eleventh day of your tour we will take you to the destination waiting you with the beach beauty. This day, you will be taking a flight to Goa via Mumbai. Goa is one of the most famous beach tourism destinations of the country. Lakhs of tourists arrive here every year at any point of time in order to witness the chill of beaches and beauty of Churches exhibited here in this city. After arriving in Goa, we will provide you a comfortable stay in a recommended hotel.

The twelfth day of the tour will be purely dedicated to exploring the city of Goa. This day will include your visit to different beaches and Churches in the city.

After having breakfast in the Goa hotel, you will be reaching airport for your onwards journey.
