5 NIGHTS / 6 DAYS - MUMBAI AURANGABAD INDIA TOUR

Destination

Mumbai - Aurangabad - Mumbai

The handicrafts, the architecture and the all over look of palaces and museums based in India have always been respected for the art it exhibits. For past so many years, the country has added millions to its admirers’ list just because it is rich with its art and architecture. Here to make you believe the same thing, we offer you a trip full of Indian art where the oldest monuments and caves speaks about the stories lost somewhere in the history books.

Itinerary

Unlike other tour packages, this one starts from Mumbai, the city which recognised as the biggest financial hub of India. This city has got spark in the day time and glitters in the night. Mumbai is not behind in any category; therefore we can also commence an artistic tour from here. After you arrive in Mumbai, our representative reaches the airport to recieve you and takes you to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

On the very next day after staying overnight in Mumbai hotel, we will directly take you to Aurangabad by air. After reaching there, a room in the recommended hotel will be provided to you, where you will be relaxing for the whole day. This Maharashtra based city is famous for its big and small industries, exquisite hand woven brocades and fine silken textiles. However, many in the whole world know this place for its nearby Ajanta and Ellora caves.

The third day of this tour starts with the motive of visiting famous places out of Aurangabad. Well, the first place we suggest is Ajanta Caves. These caves are based in inner fold of Sahardi Hills and are 100 Kms far from Aurangabad. These caves were discovered in the year 1819 by the group of British army officers. This also features some chapels and monasteries. After paying a pleasing visit to Ajanta Caves, we will return to Aurangabad for an overnight stay.

Coming to the fourth day of tour, we take you to the second famous place near Aurangabad and that is Ellora Caves. At this day you don’t have to travel a lot as Ellora Caves are based at just 30 Kms distance from Aurangabad. This place attracts you with the caves by asserting different religions in its different parts. Here 12 caves are dedicated to Mahayana Buddhist, 17 are Hindu Caves, 5 caves resembles Jainism, while addition 22 caves are dedicated to Lord Shiva. On the same day, after experiencing the cheerful journey to Ellora Caves, your next stop is determined as Aurangabad Caves. These caves are famous for resembling TANTRIC images in its architectural designs. After this visit, we will take you to your respective hotel, where you will have an overnight stay.

An air journey for Mumbai return is scheduled for you after the breakfast on the fifth day of tour. After reaching Mumbai, you will be experiencing another cave fantasy as we will take you to the tour of very famous Elephanta Caves. The day is also scheduled for visiting numerous interesting sites of Mumbai. Here you will staying in Mumbai hotel at night.

On the sixth day of tour, you will wake up for saying a good bye to this very much artistic tour and to Mumbai also. After breakfast, our representatives will take you to airport, where you will take a flight for your home or onwards journey.
